  • 🔴 ActionPaxos = 1st SEC-registered blockchain clearing agency & CSD — the post-DTCC era opens (Jun 4) The SEC registered Paxos Securities Settlement Company (PSSC) as a clearing agency under Section 17A — the first and only blockchain-native firm approved as a registered CSD in the US (18-month temporary registration, 7 years of SEC engagement). PSSC ran a settlement pilot with major institutions. 💡 Montis angle: A compliant, blockchain-native CSD competitor to DTCC now exists legally inside a major regulatory perimeter — the precedent every European supervisor and counterparty will reference. Strengthens the authorisation narrative and the institutional sales case. ↗ Paxos / Federal Register · June 4

The incumbents are spending years and fortunes on modernisation Montis can offer natively. ↗ TCS / PostTrade360 · June 4
  • 🔴 ActionClearBank Europe Digital Asset Rails live — EURC + SEPA Instant 24/7, MiCA-compliant (Jun 2) ClearBank Europe launched live Digital Asset Rails: institutions convert fiat → EURC (Circle MiCA stablecoin) → transfer cross-border → redeem fiat via SEPA Instant 24/7. First Dutch bank with a MiCA notification; USDC support due H2 2026. 💡 Montis angle: The first operational, regulated cash-leg for EU institutional settlement — exactly the programmable cash layer that runs alongside a DLT securities platform. A potential interoperability partner, not just a market signal. ↗ ClearBank · June 2
